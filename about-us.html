About Us

A Breath of Fresh Air was formed by concerned individuals from varied backgrounds with the goal to raise awareness and funds for issues related to lung health and air quality. Journalists, engineers, educators, entrepreneurs, respiratory experts, writers, actors, physicians, nurses, communications and technology professionals pool skills, experience and creative resources to “do good while having a good time! With a common passion for lung health and clean air, the decision to support the American Lung Association is an easy one.

The Big Screen Bash

This annual soiree is the result of a few of our charter members brainstorming an event that would be “creative, exciting and unusual” while raising money for a worthy charity.

Past Years Events​

2014- Snips, Ships and Oompa Loompas: Featuring the Films of Johnny Depp, at The Olmsted

2013- Dial M for Marvelous: A Silver Screen Soiree Celebrating Hitchcock, at The Olmsted

2012- Here’s Lookin’ at You, Kid: A Celebration of 1940′s Silver Screen Stars, at the historic Brown Hotel

2011- An Evening at the Blind Tiger: A Sold-out Speakeasy, at the historic Louisville Water Tower

2010- Modern Woman Meets Medieval Man, at the Frazier History Museum

2009- Celtic Samhain Festival
