Live Auction

Below are some of the exciting items that will be auctioned off at this year's event!
Please keep checking back as we add new items.

Bring 211 Clover Lane into Your Own Home

Invite some of your closest friends to join you in experiencing some of Louisville’s finest dining… prepared in the intimacy of your own home. Andrew Smith, owner of 211 Clover Lane will provide a four-course culinary dinner for eight.

Dining and Tour of Old Louisville with Historian Tom Owen

Enjoy a social hour, historical walking tour of Old Louisville and dinner with Tom Owen in the Old Armstrong Mansion. This once-in-a-lifetime experience for eight includes a gourmet four-course dinner with one of Louisville’s most entertaining historians!

Casa de Cielo – Pure Luxury in the Mayan Riviera

This elegant beachfront villa in a private gated community can be yours for a week. Amenities in this 2-story “house in the sky” include four spacious bedrooms with large in-suite baths, private swimming pool, kayaks, patios, dining and living room, and fully-equipped kitchen offering everything needed to prepare and serve an elegant gourmet dinner (or have the dinner prepared for you by a professional cook). Enjoy drinks while viewing ocean sunsets from the roof-top patio or quietly slip to the beach after dark – you may catch a sea turtle coming to shore to lay her eggs. Truly a trip to tropical paradise in Akumal, Mexico.
