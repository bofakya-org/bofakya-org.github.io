About Us

A Breath of Fresh Air was formed by concerned individuals from varied backgrounds with the goal to raise awareness and funds for issues related to lung health and air quality. Journalists, engineers, educators, entrepreneurs, respiratory experts, writers, actors, physicians, nurses, communications and technology professionals pool skills, experience and creative resources to “do good while having a good time! With a common passion for lung health and clean air, the decision to support the American Lung Association is an easy one.

The Big Screen Bash

This annual soiree is the result of a few of our charter members brainstorming an event that would be “creative, exciting and unusual” while raising money for a worthy charity.

Our Team

Ty Adams

Ty Adams is glad to see heightened awareness of air quality issues and positive outcomes both regionally and nationally. He is a member of A Breath of Fresh Air's Advisory Committee, a Team Coach for ALA's inaugural 2014 ClimbLouisville and assists in a variety of tasks for the October Gala (aka: Go-pher). In his spare time he is a design entrepreneur and managing director of A:KDesign working on fun & innovative products.

Deena Adams

Deena Adams is Development Manager for American Lung Association in Kentucky and is a charter member of A Breath of Fresh Air. She served as the group's co-director and from 2009 - 2012 co-chaired BOFA's Big Screen Bash. Deena's experience with pulmonary dysfunction is personal and she hopes to influence positive change in Kentuckiana's air quality and lung health.
